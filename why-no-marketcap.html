Why Does This Token Have No Market Cap or Rank?

Some tokens on CoinGecko show no market cap or ranking. This is usually due to one of the following reasons:

1. No Circulating Supply Provided

CoinGecko requires a public circulating supply to calculate market cap. If the project hasn’t provided this, market cap and rank will be blank.

2. New or Illiquid Token

If the token is recently launched or trades on low-liquidity pools, CoinGecko may not list full metrics until more reliable data is available.

3. Missing or Incorrect Token Data

If contract details, logos, or metadata are missing or wrong, CoinGecko may disable full tracking until it’s corrected.

4. Wrapped, Derivative, or Inactive Asset

Some tokens represent staked assets or are inactive, and therefore don’t get a separate rank or market cap.

5. Under Review

CoinGecko may be manually reviewing the token for eligibility, especially if it's a recent listing or suspected spam.

If you're a developer or token team, submit missing info through the CoinGecko Request Form.
